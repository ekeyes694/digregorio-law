Our Location

As of August of 2014, we relocated our offices to Suite M of the professional offices at 769 Plain Street (which is Route 139) in Marshfield. Our new home is peaceful and private with ample off-street parking. From Route 3, take Exit 12 then proceed east on Route 139. You will pass through a traffic light at the entrance to The Christmas Tree Shop. With Starbucks later on your left, take a right onto Enterprise Drive at the traffic light then turn right immediately into our parking lot. If you reach Roche Brothers or Marshfield High School, you have travelled a bit too far.
