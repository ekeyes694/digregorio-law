Jennifer DiGregorio

Jennifer J. DiGregorio graduated Summa Cum Laude from Springfield College and from Northeastern University School of Law in 1992. She began her legal career as an associate for Atwood and Cherny, PC, a domestic relations boutique in Boston’s Back Bay, before working as a staff attorney and later as managing attorney for Legal Services for Cape Cod & Islands, Inc. in Hyannis and Plymouth. Jennifer returned to private practice in 1997, first as a solo practitioner then as a founding partner of Caparell & DiGregorio’s Family Law Group where she practiced from 1998 to 2006. She started her own firm in the summer of 2006. Jennifer has presented seminars on family law issues for many local agencies, including the Plymouth County District Attorney’s Office, South Shore Women’s Center, and South Shore Housing Authority. She also has served on educational panels for the Plymouth County Bar Association. Jennifer is past president of the Plymouth District Bar Association and a past member of the Board of Directors for the Cape Cod Center for Women, Inc., in Falmouth. She is trained in collaborative law as well as mediation. Jennifer serves as guardian ad litem and as parent coordinator, most recently handling many cases involving requests to remove children from Massachusetts.
