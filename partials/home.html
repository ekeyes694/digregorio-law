About

Bringing almost 30 years of collective legal experience, we ensure families and the individual what they deserve. Helping navigate through the emotional, financial, and legal challenges created by divorce or separation.

Our Work

Working as a team with the client we bring hard work with creative approaches, and the respect you deserve. We waste no time and make you feel like you're in the right hands that will get the job done.

Our Approach

We offer an innovative approach well-suited to temper the emotionally and financially difficult issues presented in domestic relation cases. It blends the best features of mediation with the power of a zealous individual.
